About

Summing is the electronic music project of Chris Lawrence, a musician from Newcastle, Australia. His first album, Distant Cousin, was released in July 2013. He has had the privelage of sharing the stage with the likes of Lovely Head, Tim Fitz, King Single and Yon Yonson. Chris was once upon a time the guitarist and singer for Long Island Sound.
